Airbnb in Lexington, KY

In recent years, short-term rentals (STRs) have come under increased scrutiny for their impact on the cities in which they operate. Use this map to explore the footprint of Airbnb – the largest STR platform currently operating – in Lexington, KY.


This map draws inspiration from InsideAirbnb, a data visualization project for assessing the impact of STRs across different cities in the world. By including key indicators of neighborhood change, this project provides a more holistic picture of the relationship between STRs and gentrification.
